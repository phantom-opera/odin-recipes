Coquito

Description

Coquito is a creamy, tropical, Puerto Rican holiday drink with tons of coconut flavor. It's made with both evaporated and condensed milk, cream of coconut, spices, rum, and — in this version — egg yolks.

Ingredients

  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 2 large egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 can cream of coconut
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup white rum
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
